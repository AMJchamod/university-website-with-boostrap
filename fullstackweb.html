Cybersecurity and ethical HACKING

Additional Information

Duration: 6 months

Lectures: 2

Quizzes: 820

Price (LKR):20000

Curriculum

Understanding Cybersecurity: Overview of the field, importance, and key concepts.
History of Cybersecurity: Evolution of cyber threats and defense mechanisms.
Cybersecurity Landscape: Current trends and future outlook.

2. Cyber Threats and Attacks

Types of Cyber Attacks: Phishing, malware, ransomware, DDoS, etc.
Case Studies of Major Cyber Attacks: Analyzing real-world incidents.
Attack Vectors: How cyber attacks are carried out.

3. Network Security

Fundamentals of Network Security: Key principles and protocols.
Securing Network Infrastructure: Firewalls, intrusion detection/prevention systems.
Virtual Private Networks (VPNs): Usage and implementation.

4. Ethical Hacking and Penetration Testing

Ethical Hacking Basics: Principles and legal considerations.
Penetration Testing Techniques: Tools and methodologies.
Hands-on Labs: Practical exercises in a controlled environment.

5. Cybersecurity Tools and Technologies

Security Information and Event Management (SIEM): Overview and tools.
Antivirus and Anti-Malware Software: Functionality and usage.
Encryption Techniques: Ensuring data confidentiality.

6. Web Application Security

Common Web Vulnerabilities: SQL injection, XSS, CSRF, etc.
Secure Coding Practices: Writing secure code to prevent vulnerabilities.
Web Application Firewalls (WAFs): Implementing and configuring.

7. Cybersecurity for Cloud Computing

Cloud Security Fundamentals: Key principles and challenges.
Securing Cloud Services: Strategies for IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS.
Identity and Access Management (IAM): Best practices and tools.

8. Incident Response and Management

Incident Response Planning: Creating and implementing an incident response plan.
Forensic Analysis: Investigating and analyzing security breaches.
Disaster Recovery and Business Continuity: Ensuring resilience.

9. Cybersecurity Governance and Compliance

Cybersecurity Frameworks and Standards: NIST, ISO 27001, GDPR, etc.
Compliance Requirements: Legal and regulatory considerations.
Risk Management: Identifying and mitigating cybersecurity risks.

10. Emerging Trends in Cybersecurity

Artificial Intelligence and Cybersecurity: Leveraging AI for defense.
Blockchain Security: Securing blockchain applications.
IoT Security: Challenges and solutions for Internet of Things devices.

11. Capstone Project

Real-World Cybersecurity Challenge: Applying learned skills to a practical problem.
Project Presentation: Presenting findings and solutions to peers and instructors.
